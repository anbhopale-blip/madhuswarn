Our Story

Born From A Love Of
The Forest & Its Gifts

Founded by Avadhut Yadav, entrepreneur and organic farmer, with a vision to bring pure Sahyadri forest honey and authentic natural products directly from nature to families. Madhuswarn Naturals started with a simple dream — to bring the purest honey from the heart of Sahyadri to families across India. What began as a small family operation in the hills of Maharashtra has grown into a trusted name for raw, unprocessed forest honey.

2018
The Beginning — Started our journey with pure Sahyadri forest honey, serving local families with naturally harvested, unadulterated honey and other organic products
2024
Growing Community — Earned the trust of 500+ families through quality products and word-of-mouth recommendations.
2025
Product Expansion — Expanded beyond honey to offer pure desi ghee and other carefully selected organic products.
2026
Government Recognition — Honored with a Government Agricultural Award for excellence in natural honey production and sustainable practices.
2026
Going Digital — Launched our official website, making authentic organic products accessible to customers across Maharashtra.
